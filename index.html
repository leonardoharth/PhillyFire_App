Welcome! Here is the guide to use the App:

* When opening up, if the hydrant points are not shown on the map, or if you could only see fire engine polygons, please refresh the page.

On the map

  • When opening the page, you will see on the map, hydrant points are overlaid with engine polygons that are colored based on fire risk. You are free to use "Map Views" dropdown button to select which feature(s) you would like to see.
  • Toggling the "Hydrant opacity" and "Engine opacity" sliders can adjust the transparency level of hydrant points and fire engines appearance on the map.
  • Clicking on each hydrant point or each engine polygon will show a popup window that display its basic information. However, since there are over 25000 hydrant points with a high density shown on the map, if you would like to check information for individual hydrants, it will be better for you to turn off the engine layer ahead of time by clicking "Map Views" dropdown botton and de-select "engines" .

On the sidebar: Customize hydrant inspection prioritization and views

  • If you want to build hydrant inspection prioritization, in Factors to prioritize, you need to check at least one of the four factors; you are also free to check two or three or all factors with any combinations.
  • After you make selections, you can directly click "Update map" to see how different hydrant priorities based on your customization distribute across the City. The priorities are score from 1 to 10, 1 is the top priority and 10 is the lowest priority. If you want to check a specific value of priority, you need to check the "Filter by priority?" box first, and then toggle the slider to choose the priority value you want to see. Finally, you then click "Update map" button to see the results on the map.
  • If you want to further zoom into a specifc fire engine to see how its hydrant priority looks like, after you make selections in "Factors to prioritize", you can choose to skip "Filter by priority" part and check "Filter by engine?"box . Clicking the "See engines" button will allow you to see a map with different engines labeled with engine numbers. You can then type the engine number you want to zoom in by referring to the map.
  • If you want to check a certain value of hydrant inspection priority in a certain engine, after you make selections in "Factors to prioritize", you need to select AND complete both "Filter by priority" part and "Filter by engine" part. Then click "Update map".
  • If you only want to see hydrant points in a certain engine, you can skip "Factors to prioritize" and "Filter by priority", and check "Filter by engine" box and put the engine number you want.
  • "Reset map" button will clear all selections and/or input you've made and reset all hydrant points on the map into the default color.
